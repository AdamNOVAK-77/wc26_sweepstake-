Sweepstake points are earned by every team you hold. Group stage: win or shootout win +3, draw +1, loss 0. Knockout wins escalate — Round of 32 +4, Round of 16 +6, Quarter-final +8, Semi-final +12, Final +16 (third-place play-off +4), so a deep run can swing the table. A shootout win counts as a win for that round. Live games score in real time — provisional points (marked in red) update as matches play and lock in at full time. The ▲▼ next to each name shows how their position has moved since you last opened the table on this device. Group tables use standard 3/1/0 with points, goal difference, goals scored, then fair play (fewest cards: yellow 1, red 3), matching FIFA. Card data comes from manual entry. Results and goal scorers update automatically from the openfootball public-domain dataset (free, no sign-up). Full-time scores and scorers appear once each match finishes; in-play minute-by-minute scores aren’t included, so a game in progress shows a LIVE marker until its result lands. Use “Add a result by hand” to get ahead, log cards, or set penalty-shootout winners, which aren’t detected automatically.
